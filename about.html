About Us

At Legacy Credit and Gas Cards, we specialize in providing seamless and reliable credit solutions tailored to the unique needs of businesses with fleet fueling requirements. Our company gas cards are designed to empower fleet managers and business owners with a secure, efficient, and cost-effective way to manage fuel expenses.

With our tagline, “Company Gas Cards For Fleet Fueling,” we are committed to delivering solutions that enhance operational efficiency, reduce administrative burdens, and ensure smooth fueling for your fleet.

Based in South Burlington, Vermont, our headquarters at 3164 Buck Drive is the hub of our innovation and customer service excellence. Led by Anthony Randall, our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure your business enjoys maximum value and convenience.

For more information, feel free to reach out to us at a.randall@legacycreditcard.net. Let us fuel your success!
