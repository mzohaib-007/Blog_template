Card Services

At Legacy Credit and Gas Cards, we understand the unique challenges fleet managers face when it comes to managing fuel expenses. That’s why we offer tailored solutions to simplify your operations and maximize your savings. Our company gas cards and credit cards for gas purchases are designed to give you control, flexibility, and cost efficiency.

Benefits of Our Gas Cards and Credit Solutions

1. Cost Savings

With our gas cards, you can unlock significant savings for your business:

  • Discounted Fuel Rates: Enjoy exclusive fuel discounts at participating stations, reducing your overall fuel costs.
  • Tax Reporting Simplified: Easily track and manage fuel tax exemptions for eligible purchases, saving time and money.
  • Eliminated Unauthorized Purchases: Our cards include purchase controls, ensuring that fuel purchases align with your business needs.

2. Enhanced Expense Management

Our solutions streamline how you manage fuel expenses:

  • Detailed Reporting: Gain insights into fuel consumption and spending with detailed transaction reports.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Track transactions in real-time, giving you greater control over your fleet’s fuel usage.
  • Customizable Limits: Set spending limits for individual drivers to avoid unnecessary expenditures.

3. Improved Efficiency

Our solutions streamline how you manage fuel expenses:

  • Accepted Nationwide: Our gas cards are accepted at thousands of fuel stations nationwide, ensuring your fleet can refuel wherever they go.
  • Streamlined Administration: Consolidate all fleet fuel expenses into one easy-to-manage account.
  • Faster Reconciliation: Simplify your bookkeeping with detailed, consolidated billing statements.
