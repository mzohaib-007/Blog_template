ADA Compliance Statement

At Legacy Credit and Gas Cards, we are committed to ensuring accessibility for all individuals, including those with disabilities. We strive to provide a website that is user-friendly and accessible to everyone, in compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and other applicable accessibility standards.

Our Accessibility Efforts

To make our website accessible, we are actively working to:

  • Follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level.
  • Provide text alternatives for non-text content, such as images and multimedia.
  • Ensure our website is navigable by keyboard and compatible with screen readers.
  • Use clear and concise language to make our content easier to understand.
  • Test our website regularly to identify and address any accessibility issues.

Ongoing Commitment

We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we are continually improving the user experience for everyone. If you encounter any accessibility barriers while using our website, please let us know so we can address them promptly.
